    • Powder Coating

    Powder coating is a type of coating that is applied as a free-flowing, dry powder. It furnishes a more stable finish than liquid paints can offer, while still providing an attractive finish.

    Wood Finish Coating

    Wood finish is typically made with a solution of nitrocellulose and solvents to make a glossy or matte coating. Frequently sprayed on, it leaves a thin coat that dries faster than other finishes.

    PVDF Coating

    It is applied as a liquid spray. It is typically applied electrostatically and then cured under heat. It is widely used commercial and industrial coatings on the market.
